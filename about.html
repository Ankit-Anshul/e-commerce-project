Who We Are?

Ecommerce can feel a little impersonal at times. Shoppers experience your brand through a screen instead of in person, where they can touch and handle the products before making a purchase. As a result of this detachment, shoppers might forget your brand or even mix you up with a competitor. To avoid this, it’s important to make a strong impression on every visitor. One excellent way to leave a mark is through your About Us page. Scrolling down, readers encounter a section where they can flip through the different steps Leesa takes in creating a mattress. Communicate the story of your business and why you started it. Describe the customers or the cause that your business serves. Explain your business model or how your products are made. Put a face to your business, featuring the founders or the people on your team.
